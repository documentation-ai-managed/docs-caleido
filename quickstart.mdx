## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- An active email address for account creation
- Access to your accounting software (QuickBooks Online, Xero, or similar)
- Admin permissions in your accounting platform for integrations
</Callout>

Caleido provides real-time cash forecasting by connecting to your financial data. Complete these steps to set up your account and generate your first forecast in under 10 minutes.

## Create Your Caleido Account

Follow these steps to sign up and log in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.caleido.io` and click **Sign Up**.

    Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address.

    <Image
      src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/DkIuGH7O9oIT166snTUErTcbE.jpg?width=2556&height=1724"
      alt="Caleido signup screen"
      width="800"
      height="538"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://app.caleido.io/login` and sign in with your credentials.

    Complete any onboarding prompts, such as setting your company name and time zone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your Accounting Integration

Connect your primary accounting platform to import transactions and balances automatically. Caleido supports popular platforms like QuickBooks and Xero.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="QuickBooks Online" icon="database">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Authorize Connection" icon="link">
        In the Caleido dashboard, navigate to **Integrations > Accounting**.

        Select **QuickBooks Online** and click **Connect**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="OAuth Flow" icon="key">
        Log in to your QuickBooks account and grant Caleido read access to accounts, invoices, and bills.

        Caleido will sync your last 90 days of data automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Xero" icon="database">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Authorize Connection" icon="link">
        Go to **Integrations > Accounting** and choose **Xero**.

        Click **Connect with Xero**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Grant Permissions" icon="key">
        Approve access in Xero for bank transactions, contacts, and reports.

        Initial sync completes in 2-3 minutes.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
After connecting, verify the sync status in the dashboard. Caleido refreshes data every 15 minutes.
</Callout>

## Generate Your First Cash Forecast

With data connected, create a basic forecast.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Forecast" icon="plus">
    Click **New Forecast** in the dashboard.

    Set the forecast period to 12 months and select your connected accounting source.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Assumptions" icon="settings">
    Adjust key inputs like expected inflows from receivables and planned expenses.

    Use the default assumptions for your first run.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run Forecast" icon="play">
    Click **Generate Forecast**. Results appear in seconds, showing monthly cash balance projections.
  </Step>
</Steps>
