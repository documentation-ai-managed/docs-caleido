## Overview

Follow these simple steps to start writing in Caleido.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Caleido">
    Go to `https://app.caleido.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project">
    Click **New Project** on the dashboard. Give your project a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation">
    Inside your new project, click **Add Page** or start editing the default page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
