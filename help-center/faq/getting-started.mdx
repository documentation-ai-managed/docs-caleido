## Quick Start

To create your first documentation project:

1. Log into Caleido at `https://app.caleido.com`.
2. Click the **New Project** button on your dashboard.
3. Enter a project name and optional description.
4. Click **Create**.

Your project now appears in your workspace. You can add pages and invite team members from the project view.

## Need help?
Contact support if you have trouble creating a project.
