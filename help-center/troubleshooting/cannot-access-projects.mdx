---
title: Cannot Access Projects
description: Fix project access issues in Caleido.
---

## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" message
- Projects missing from dashboard

## Causes

- Not a member of the project
- Logged in with wrong account

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login">
    Log out and back in to ensure correct account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Ask the project owner to invite you from the Project > Members page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
